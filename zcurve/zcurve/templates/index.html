ZCURVE is an ab initio program for gene finding in bacterial or archaeal genomes and its latest version is 3.0. Based on cross validations of 422 prokaryotic genomes, ZCURVE 3.0 has slightly higher accuracy than Glimmer 3.02. This page provides access to ZCURVE 3.0.
